Introduction

The Indian Premier League, popularly known as IPL, is a professional Twenty20 cricket league in India that was founded in 2008 by the
Board of Control for Cricket in India (BCCI).
The IPL has become one of the most popular and lucrative cricket leagues in the world, attracting top players from all over the globe.
